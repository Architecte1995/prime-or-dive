# Prime or Dive

Factorize or die. 37 to 77,773,337 range. Cathedral 1995 arithmetic. Single-file HTML5. Prime = instant death.

## How to play
Open `prime-or-dive.html` in any browser (mobile or desktop). No installation, no server, no build.

**Rules:**
- Click a composite number to split it into two factors
- **Do NOT click a prime number** — instant death
- Composites reaching the bottom subtract their value from your Pool
- Goal: factorize completely while keeping your Pool positive

**Speed:** 5 randomized velocity levels. Small numbers (≤20) are always supersonic. Large numbers crawl or rush unpredictably.

## Range
- **Minimum:** 37 (the minimal prime)
- **Maximum:** 77,773,337 (the 73-stretch prime limit)
- Numbers are randomly generated between these bounds on each reload

## Technical
- Pure HTML5/CSS/ES6
- Single file, zero dependencies
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Offline playable
